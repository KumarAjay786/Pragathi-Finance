About Pragathi Financial Solutions

We are a group of qualified finance professionals having 2 decades of experience in financial services industry especially corporate debt syndication in india. We are working with mejority of the public and private sector banks, financial institutions, co operative banks, NBFCs, Real estate funds, Asset Rconstruction Companies and Asset Management companies in india and have build strong deal execution capacities. We believe that business finance should be available from the organized lending system to all eligible and deserving borrowers to pursue their business Goals and if there are any hurdles, they can be reolved through responsible and constructive advisory and representation.

We also aim to provide a solid paltform to fragmented independent finance professionals across india who can list, Outsource, consolidate or expand their practice by becoming Members of Pragathi Financial Solutions. we shall aggregate the scattered debt requirement across india under one roof and give such professionals two way opportunities to use our professional systems, debt market knowledge and execution capabilties as well as pie of our business for execution in their regions. Borrowers can directly list their loan requirements which can be executed through thousands of online and offline associates of our portal across india.

The front end lending cycle does seem to end with lending but one has to also provide Solutions for fag end of the credit cycle for healthy debt market , that is we have to provide for solution of stressed assets their revival and recovery. We again intend to bring all such stressed borrowers, lenders of the last resorts, Asset reconstruction companies, resolution professional (IPR) , recovery agents etc under one roof so that all such constutients can easily access service of others.

Pragathi Financial Solutions will serve as online common platform for finance requirement of the borrowers, lenders and advisory services of the finance professionals and allied services professionals.

We will specifically cater to the corporate loan cases where financial advisory services are required to crack the ice or better representation to lender is necessary for arranging the loans. we render our advisory based loan services for corporate loan arrangements like cash credit, overdrafts, term loans, buyers credit, Foreign Currency loans, acquisition finance, auction finance, structured finance, large ticket size unsecured loans, promoter funding, equity buy out finance, real estate or construction finance.
