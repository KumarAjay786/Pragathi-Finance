CEO's Message

Dear Clients, Partners, and Visitors,

It is with great pride that I welcome you to Pragathi Accounting and Tax Consultancy, a division of Pragathi IT Ads Pvt Ltd. For over a decade, we have been committed to delivering high-quality accounting and tax services, driven by a passion for excellence and a desire to make a meaningful impact on the financial well-being of our clients.

We aim to build lasting partnerships with our clients. We understand that every business is unique, and that’s why we take a personalized approach to each client’s.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to offer reliable and professional financial services tailored to the specific needs of businesses and individuals. Over the years, we have grown significantly, thanks to our clients' trust and the hard work of our dedicated team of seasoned accounting professionals and legal experts. Our diverse service portfolio now includes accounting, income tax return (ITR) filing, ROC (Registrar of Companies) compliance, and much more, providing a one-stop solution for all financial and regulatory needs.

At Pragathi Accounting and Tax Consultancy, we believe in more than just providing services; we aim to build lasting partnerships with our clients. We understand that every business is unique, and that’s why we take a personalized approach to each client’s requirements, ensuring that we offer solutions that best fit their goals and challenges. Our team continuously strives to stay ahead of regulatory changes and industry trends, allowing us to provide insightful, practical advice to support your business’s growth and financial health.

As we continue to grow, our focus remains on maintaining the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and professionalism in everything we do. Thank you for being a part of our journey, and we look forward to serving you for many years to come.

Warm regards,

Anil Raj Chithran CEO
Pragathi Group of Company
